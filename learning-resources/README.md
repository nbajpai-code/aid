# 🎓 AI/ML Learning Resources

The AI and Machine Learning field evolves at a rapid pace. This section curates the absolute best courses, newsletters, podcasts, and blogs to help you stay ahead, from absolute foundations to advanced AI engineering.

---

## 🏫 Structured Courses (Free & Paid)

### Neural Networks: Zero to Hero (Andrej Karpathy)
A masterclass by Andrej Karpathy (former Director of AI at Tesla and OpenAI co-founder) on building neural networks from scratch.
*   **Link**: [YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUbFy9y) / [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/karpathy/nn-zero-to-hero)
*   **Level**: Intermediate (requires basic Python/Math).
*   **What you build**: Implement backpropagation, MLP, WaveNet, and a full GPT-2 model in PyTorch from scratch.

### DeepLearning.AI (Andrew Ng)
The ultimate learning portal for AI practitioners.
*   **Link**: [deeplearning.ai](https://www.deeplearning.ai/)
*   **Notable Offerings**:
    *   **Machine Learning Specialization**: Foundational math and algorithms.
    *   **Deep Learning Specialization**: Detailed study of CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers.
    *   **AI Short Courses**: Practical 1-hour courses on Prompt Engineering, LangChain, Agentic Workflows, and RAG.

### Practical Deep Learning for Coders (Fast.ai)
A top-down course by Jeremy Howard that gets you training state-of-the-art models in Python from day one.
*   **Link**: [course.fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/)
*   **Level**: Beginner-friendly for programmers.
*   **Approach**: Focuses on code and application first, then explains the underlying mathematics.

### Hugging Face Course
An interactive course covering the Hugging Face ecosystem (Transformers, Datasets, Tokenizers, Accelerate).
*   **Link**: [huggingface.co/learn](https://huggingface.co/learn)
*   **Level**: Intermediate.
*   **What you learn**: Fine-tune models, upload weights to the Hub, and create custom pipelines.

## ✉️ Industry Newsletters & Blogs

### Latent Space
The premier newsletter and podcast for AI engineers. Written by Swyx and Alessio, it covers the emerging AI engineering stack, developer tooling, and models.
*   **Link**: [latent.space](https://www.latent.space/)

### TLDR AI
A daily, bite-sized newsletter summarizing the most important AI research, news, and developer tools.
*   **Link**: [tldr.tech/ai](https://tldr.tech/ai)

### The Batch (DeepLearning.AI)
Andrew Ng’s weekly newsletter covering both technical breakthroughs and societal impacts of AI.
*   **Link**: [deeplearning.ai/the-batch](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/)

### Hugging Face Blog
Deep technical articles, release announcements, and tutorials from the HF engineering team.
*   **Link**: [huggingface.co/blog](https://huggingface.co/blog)

## 📄 Research & Paper Hubs

- **arXiv**: The open repository for electronic preprints of scientific papers. Most AI research is posted here months before peer-reviewed journals. [arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/)
- **Hugging Face Papers**: Interactive portal to read, discuss, and upvote the latest papers with the developer community. [huggingface.co/papers](https://huggingface.co/papers)
- **Arxiv Sanity Preserver**: A tool built by Andrej Karpathy to filter, search, and recommend arXiv papers based on your interests. [arxiv-sanity-lite.com](http://www.arxiv-sanity-lite.com/)
