# 🗓️ AI Conferences & Regional Communities

Connecting with fellow builders is key to growing as an AI developer. This section curates the top global research and engineering conferences alongside active local meetups in **Washington DC** and the **San Francisco Bay Area**.

---

## 🌐 Global AI/ML Conferences (2025 / 2026)

These events represent the pinnacle of academic research and commercial applications of Artificial Intelligence.

| Conference | Focus | Core Audience | Typical Timing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NVIDIA GTC** | GPU computing, hardware, enterprise generative AI, robotics. | Developers, executives, researchers. | March |
| **NeurIPS** | Cutting-edge machine learning and neural network research. | Researchers, PhDs. | December |
| **AI Engineer Summit** | Applied AI engineering, developer tools, RAG, agentic workflows. | Software Engineers, AI Builders. | June / October |

## 🌁 San Francisco Bay Area AI Communities

The Bay Area is the epicenter of the generative AI boom, hosting multiple hackathons, meetups, and developer salons every week.

### 📍 Key Spaces & Hubs
- **AWS GenAI Loft (San Francisco)**: A physical space in SF hosting panels, startup pitches, hackathons, and hands-on workshops. [Link](https://aws.amazon.com/developer/community/loft/)
- **Shack15 (Ferry Building)**: Hosts regular discussions featuring top AI founders, researchers, and venture capitalists.

### 👥 Top SF Meetups & Groups
- **SF AI Hackers / Builders**: Active community hosting regular weekend hackathons and demo nights.
- **LlamaIndex & LangChain SF Meetups**: Focus on production RAG, agent architectures, and LLM integrations.
- **Cerebral Valley**: A community organizing invite-only hacker house events and AI summits in SF. [Website](https://cerebralvalley.ai/)

## 🏛️ Washington DC Area AI Communities

The DC metropolitan area has a rapidly growing AI ecosystem, with a unique blend of civic tech, federal integrations, defense tech, and commercial software engineering.

### 👥 Top DC Meetups & Groups
- **DC AI / Machine Learning Meetup**: One of the oldest tech meetups in the region, hosting regular talks on deep learning, NLP, and model architectures. [Meetup Link](https://www.meetup.com/)
- **GDG Washington DC & GDG Capital Region**: Hosts developer-first events (like DevFestDC) featuring dedicated tracks for Gemini, TensorFlow, and Google Cloud AI.
- **Federal AI & GovTech Meetups**: Groups focused on deploying secure, private AI models in public sector, government, and highly regulated industries.
- **Northern Virginia (NoVA) AI Builders**: Meetups focusing on cloud architectures, GPU workloads, and local LLM fine-tuning.
